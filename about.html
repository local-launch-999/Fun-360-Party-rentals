FAQ

Common Questions

How far do you travel for delivery? ▼
We are based in Bridgeport, CT and cover all of Fairfield County. Delivery within 30 miles is free. Beyond that, we charge $5 per mile. Include your location when you reach out and we will calculate the exact cost!
Is setup really included? ▼
100%! We deliver, fully set up every item, and return to break it all down when the party is over. You do not lift a finger. Just show us your space and we handle everything.
What do I need to provide? ▼
For bounce houses: a flat outdoor space and a standard outdoor electrical outlet within 50 feet. For the pool combo: also a garden hose. For the foam cannon: a garden hose and outlet. We bring everything else!
Do I need to pay a deposit? ▼
Yes — a deposit is required to reserve your date and lock in your rentals. The deposit amount is included in your custom quote. It goes toward your total and the balance is due on the day of your event.
Are the bounce houses safe? ▼
Absolutely. All inflatables are commercial-grade, regularly cleaned and inspected. We secure them properly at setup. Adult supervision is required at all times and we will walk you through safety guidelines when we arrive.
What if it rains? ▼
We monitor weather and contact you ahead of your event. Bounce houses cannot be used in rain or high winds. We will work with you on rescheduling if needed — another reason adding a tent is always a smart choice!
How do I pay? ▼
We accept Cash, Zelle, and Venmo. The deposit is due when you confirm your booking and the remaining balance is due when we arrive on party day.
