FAQ

Common Questions

How far do you travel for delivery? ▼
We are based in Bridgeport, CT and cover all of Fairfield County. Delivery within 30 miles is free. Beyond that, we charge $5 per mile. Just include your location in your quote request and we will calculate it for you!
Is setup really included? ▼
100%! We deliver, fully set up every item, and return to break it all down when the party is over. You do not need to lift a finger!
What do I need to provide for the bounce houses? ▼
A flat outdoor space and access to a standard outdoor electrical outlet within about 50 feet. For the bounce house with the mini pool, you will also need a garden hose. We bring everything else!
How does cotton candy and popcorn pricing work? ▼
Base pricing covers up to 35 guests for both machines. If your party is bigger, we add a per-person charge for the extra guests. Just give us your headcount and we will include it in your quote.
Do I need to pay a deposit? ▼
Yes — a deposit is required to officially reserve your date. The amount will be in your custom quote and goes toward your total. Remaining balance is due on the day of your event.
Are the bounce houses safe? ▼
Absolutely. All inflatables are commercial-grade, regularly cleaned and inspected. We secure them properly during setup. Adult supervision should be maintained at all times and we will walk you through safety rules on arrival.
What if it rains on my party day? ▼
We monitor weather closely and will contact you ahead of your event. Bounce houses cannot be used in rain or high winds for safety. We will work with you on rescheduling if needed — that is also why adding a tent is a great idea!
How do I pay? ▼
We accept Cash, Zelle, and Venmo. The deposit is sent at booking confirmation and the balance is due when we arrive on party day.
