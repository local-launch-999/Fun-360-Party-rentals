FAQ

Common Questions

How far do you travel for delivery? ▼
We are based in Bridgeport, CT and cover all of Fairfield County. Delivery within 30 miles is free. Beyond that, we charge $5 per mile. Include your location in your quote request and we will calculate the exact fee!
Is setup really included? ▼
100%! We deliver, fully set up every item, and return to break it all down when the party is over. You do not need to lift a finger. Just point us to your space and we handle everything.
What do I need to provide for the bounce houses? ▼
A flat outdoor space and access to a standard outdoor electrical outlet within about 50 feet. For the bounce house with the mini pool, you will also need access to a garden hose. We bring everything else!
How does cotton candy and popcorn pricing work? ▼
Base pricing for both machines covers up to 35 guests. If your party has more than 35 people, we add a per-person charge for the additional guests. Just give us your headcount when booking and we will factor it into your quote.
Do I need to pay a deposit? ▼
Yes — a deposit is required to officially reserve your date and hold your rentals. The deposit amount will be in your custom quote. It goes toward your total balance and the remaining amount is due on the day of the event.
Are the bounce houses safe? ▼
Absolutely. All our inflatables are commercial-grade, regularly cleaned, and inspected. We secure them properly during setup. We ask that an adult supervises at all times and we walk you through safety rules when we arrive.
What if it rains on my party day? ▼
We monitor weather closely and will reach out ahead of your event. Bounce houses cannot be used in rain or high winds for safety. We will work with you on rescheduling if weather is an issue — adding a tent is always a great idea just in case!
How do I pay? ▼
We accept Cash, Zelle, and Venmo. The deposit is sent when you confirm your booking and the remaining balance is due when we arrive on party day.
